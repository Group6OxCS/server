Basic Structure

function control(car)
    -- Your code
end

Sensors

car.speed: Current car speed
car.obstacle_detection_left: Distance to obstacles to the left of the car
car.obstacle_detection_center: Distance to obstacles in front of the car
car.obstacle_detection_right: Distance to obstacles to the right of the car
car.waypoint_distance: Distance to next waypoint
car.waypoint_bearing: Bearing to next waypoint

Controls

car.forward: Go forward
car.backward: Go backward
car.left: Go left
car.right: Go right

Notes

print outputs to the console, so you will need to open your dev tools so view your script's output.
