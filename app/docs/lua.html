Basic Structure

function control(car)
    -- Your code
end

Controls

car.steering: number: Steering direction. -1 for left, 1 for right
car.acceleration: number: Acceleration. 1 for full forward, -1 for full reverse
car.brake: number: Break. 1 for full braking, 0 for no braking
car.nitro: number: Nitro boost. 1 for full boost, 0 for no boost
car.message: string: Debugging message, displayed at top

Sensors

car.speed: number: Current car speed
car.obstacle_detection_far_left: number: Distance to obstacles to the far left of the car (-45 to -20 degrees)
car.obstacle_detection_left: number: Distance to obstacles to the left of the car (-20 to -2 degrees)
car.obstacle_detection_center: number: Distance to obstacles in front of the car (-2 to 2 degrees)
car.obstacle_detection_right: number: Distance to obstacles to the right of the car (2 to 20 degrees)
car.obstacle_detection_far_right: number: Distance to obstacles to the far right of the car (20 to 45 degrees)
car.waypoint_distance: number: Distance to next waypoint
car.waypoint_bearing: number: Bearing to next waypoint
car.future_waypoint_distance: number: Distance to waypoint after the current one
car.future_waypoint_bearing: number: Bearing to waypoint after the current one

Notes

print outputs to the console, so you will need to open your dev tools so view your script's output. Using car.message may be better.
