Basic Structure

function control(car)
    -- Your code
end

Sensors

car.speed: float: Current car speed
car.obstacle_detection_far_left: float: Distance to obstacles to the far left of the car (-45 to -20 degrees)
car.obstacle_detection_left: float: Distance to obstacles to the left of the car (-20 to -2 degrees)
car.obstacle_detection_center: float: Distance to obstacles in front of the car (-2 to 2 degrees)
car.obstacle_detection_right: float: Distance to obstacles to the right of the car (2 to 20 degrees)
car.obstacle_detection_far_right: float: Distance to obstacles to the far right of the car (20 to 45 degrees)
car.waypoint_distance: float: Distance to next waypoint
car.waypoint_bearing: float: Bearing to next waypoint
car.future_waypoint_distance: float: Distance to waypoint after the current one
car.future_waypoint_bearing: float: Bearing to waypoint after the current one

Controls

car.forward: boolean: Go forward
car.backward: boolean: Go backward
car.left: boolean: Go left
car.right: boolean: Go right
car.handbreak: boolean: Break
car.message: any, converted to string: Debugging message, displayed at top

Notes

print outputs to the console, so you will need to open your dev tools so view your script's output.
